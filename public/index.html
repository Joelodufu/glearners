LEARNING AIDS SUPPORT SERVICES

G-Learn Foundation assists Learners with varieties of learning materials and technologies. Learning aids such as Exercise Books, Mathematical Sets, Textbooks, Writing Materials are offered to Learners at Basic and Secondary Education levels, while Textbooks, Electronic Tablets, Android Phones and Laptop Computers are offered to Learners at the Higher Education levels, respectively.

GLOBAL LEARNING EXCELLENCE MERIT AWARD

G-Learn Foundation works with communities to identify institutions, scholars and community stakeholders who make outstanding contributions to educational advancement and learning, human capital development, social emancipation and care for the underprivileged persons at the community level, and confers on them the prestigious G-Learn Foundation Global Learning Excellence Merit Awards (GLEMA). This is done primarily to encourage educational development at the grassroots.

















TEACHER TRAINING AND CAPACITY-BUILDING

Educational system as a sub-set of the social system is dynamic, continuously evolving and developing in its complexity and practice. Teachers, therefore, need to be developed and equipped continually to be able to cope with modern realities of teaching and learning. G-Learn Teacher Training & Capacity-Building [GTTC] has organized several in-house seminars, conferences & workshops on relevant contemporary instructional techniques and pedagogy through which teacher competence and skills have been greatly improved.
