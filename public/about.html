Click “about Here” and fill in the required details. Note; You can about as many of your artisans as you want to. Once that is done, we will reach out to your aboutd artisan and request that they sign up on our website.

Registration/Sign-up

We will reach out to your artisans and tell them how to sign up for the competition. During sign up, each artisan will be required to upload pictures of some work they have done in the past. This submission will form the basis on which screening will be done. They are advised to upload their best work.









>








Screening By Our Judges

We have carefully selected credible industry professionals to be Judges for the awards. These judges carefully screen each entry made by contestants with the aim of selecting 10 of the best in each field. The selected entries are then presented to the public.

Public Voting

Having been selected by the jury short-listed artisans will be put to a public vote on the website, a video pitch will be prepared for each short-listed artisan, and their entries will be put to a public vote.









>








Awards Ceremony.

After the voting is done and the winners have been determined, an awards ceremony where winners will be announced, and awards will be presented will be done to celebrate the excellent craftspersons.
