WHO WE ARE

GLOBAL LEARNING EMPOWERMENT AND SCHOLARSHIP FOUNDATION (G-LEARN FOUNDATION) is an autonomous, nob-religious, non-political and not-for-profit Incorporated Trustees (NGO) of education professionals, comprising Teachers, School Administrators, Educational Consultants, Researchers, Educational Leaders, Educational Service Providers and Education Policy analysts, who have raised the concern for improved learning achievement as a means of encouraging educational advancement particularly in the less developed countries (LDCs) of the world.

The mission of G-Learn Foundation is to promote learning opportunities, hence developing human capacity for self-sustenance, self-sufficiency and self-reliance.

Our target audience/clients comprise learners and potential learners at all levels of education, teachers and educational institutions.

Our focal constituencies are communities in the less developed countries of Africa. Thus, the overall concern of G-Learn Foundation is the attainment of learning achievements all its ramifications.

INCORPORATION

G-Learn Foundation was incorporated on 29th April, 2016 at Abuja, Nigeria, in strict compliance with the provisions of the Companies and Allied Matters Act No.1, 1990, Part C, Laws of the Federation of Nigeria, with Registration No.CAC/IT/86832.

G-LEARN GOVERNANCE

G-Learn Foundation is governed by a seven (7)-member Board of Trustees comprising a crop of seasoned educationists chaired by a serving Nigerian Professor, while the Management Board is led by the Director-General, a seasoned educationist with over twenty-four (24) years of education-based active work experience as a teacher, educational administrator, educational service leader, researcher and educational policy analyst.
